About Us

“JKT Myanmar International” is the corporation of “Ohio Myanmar International Co., Ltd.” and “Hokkaido Arbeit Co., Ltd. (HAJ)”

The vocational training center of JKT Myanmar International Co., Ltd. gives advanced on-job training based on education with the real work environment. To improve the friendship between Japan and Myanmar, to exchange culture, to increase corporate business and to enhance tourism, we are planning to do Japan-Myanmar business consultant and agency, and tourism business. Moreover, to support not only human resources but also technological needs in Myanmar, there are plans to create part-time jobs for students from IT and technological universities. JKT Myanmar International Co., Ltd, has been started with the aim of future business development in Myanmar, the friendship between Japan and Myanmar and the answer to the insufficiency of job opportunities for youths.

About OHIO Myanmar International Co., Ltd.

OHIO Myanmar International Co., Ltd. was established in 2014. It is a Japanese language school that has already produced great human resources such as scholarship students and employees in Japan. OHIO has collaborated with Hokkaido Arbeit Co., Ltd. (HAJ), which is located in Japan, and started JKT Myanmar International Co., Ltd. as a corporate company in 2019.

About Hokkaido Arbeit Co., Ltd. (HAJ)

Hokkaido Arbeit Johosha (hereinafter, referred to as "HAJ") has supported a variety of support activities by supporting municipalities, enterprises in Hokkaido and active young people since the establishment.
